About Us

The idea of this web application was inspired by ReRoll, a restaurant near Campus where you can play board games and role games. It is necessary because it is hard for the users to reserve a table, it serves as marketing as well, and as a control tool for the restaurant to help serve faster their customers and know exactly how many people will be seated in each table and what games will be used.

Vision

The application will show to each user his data when login in, for example, his reservation for N number of people on Friday that week and that they will play Risk, also he could look the food menu and order from the app or just tell his order to the waiter by previously watching the menu on the app.
